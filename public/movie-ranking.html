The Best Movies According to Saabik

My top 3 movies of all-time

1. Leo

Things start to take an awry turn for a mild-mannered cafe owner, who gets caught in the crosshairs of a drug cartel.

2. Premalu

Premalu is a 2024 Indian Malayalam-language romantic comedy film directed by Girish A. D. and produced by Bhavana Studios, in the company of Fahadh Faasil and Friends and Working Class Hero.

3. Hi Nanna

A doting father and his 6-year-old daughter find their lives taking a dramatic turn when the woman he loves marries someone else.
